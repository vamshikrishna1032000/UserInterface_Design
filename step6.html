instructions to run it

  • Firstly, you can see the welcome page and click on signup.
  • If you already have an account, you can log in by clicking on login.
  • You will be navigated to the home page, where you have multiple options on the left sidebar.
  • If you want to log out at any point, you can click on logout at the top right.
  • If you want to know about the app at any point, you can click on About Us at the top right.
  • My Recipes on the left have the recipes uploaded by the user. Each recipe in My Recipes can be edited by clicking on the edit icon.
  • You can upload a new recipe by clicking on Upload on the left.
  • Saved and Favorites on the left consist of the favorited and saved recipes of the user.
  • By clicking on any recipe from Saved, Favorites, and My Recipes, you can further see the recipe details, on which you can comment, favorite, save, and share.
  • On the home page, there is a search bar at the top, in which you can write your queries, apply filters, and search. You will get results according to that.
