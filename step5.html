instructions to run it

  • Firstly you can see the login page where you can enter your email and password to login. If you donâ€™t have an account then click on register to create your account. You can register by entering your name, email and password. Once you register, you can login to the website.
  • The homepage of the website appears once you login. Click on filter which gives you various options to choose from. When you click on veg, different recipes appear. Click on the onion samosa and the receipe page will appear. You can share the recipe by clicking the copy link. You can navigate back to the home page by clicking on the home icon on the left hand side corner.
  • When you click on my recipes, all the receipes that you have uploaded appears. Click on the edit icon above the recipe to edit the recipe then click on save and it will be edited successfully.
  • When you click on upload, the upload page will apper where you can fill all the details and upload the recipe. The upload successful message will appear once you click on upload.
  • To give feedback, click on feedback and the feedback form will be visible where you can write your feedback.
  • On clicking the logout button, you will be redirected to the login page.
